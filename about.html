About us

The I.L. Laboratorio Familiar N.V. was founded in 1986 as a as the first private medical laboratory established in Aruba, Dutch Caribbean by the proprietor and Director Lic. Irene van der Linde.
The laboratory provides general medical laboratory services to alleviate the shortage of laboratory services availability from the government laboratory on the island and to facilitate better service to the public by providing results within 24 hours. As a result of this innovation in services, and due to the fact that the government laboratory was not able to manage the increased demand for fast laboratory services, I.L. Laboratorio Familiar N.V. was able to enforce its rights to existence.
In 1987 I.L. Laboratorio Familiar N.V. opened a branch in San Nicolas to facilitate the patients that lives in the neighboring area. Bringing laboratory services closer to the patient was a breakthrough in the healthcare of Aruba.
It was in 1988 that, with the assistance of the Public Health Department of Curacao and the Director of Aruba’s Public Health Department, the laboratory attained the status of an “N.V.”, or a limited entity, and the first Laboratory to receive such governmental approval.
Management was aware of the fact that better and faster services to the demanding medical environment would require bigger and more strategically located facilities.
Through the years the I.L. Laboratorio Familiar N.V. expanded its services towards the rural areas and established several specimen collecting centers in these areas to facilitate the patients from outside the town areas a much easier and quicker access to the Laboratory. Centers were established in Santa Cruz, Oranjestad, San Nicolas, Pos Chiquito, Tanki Flip, Madiki and Paradera where blood and other specimen are collected and then transported to the main laboratory building. But a much special service that I.L. Laboratorio Familiar N.V. introduced was the blood collection at home, which was very needed by bed-ridden patients.
In 2003 the Laboratory moved to its present location in De La Sallestraat 72 in Oranjestad, now with a larger staff. This building is equipped with a large number of the most modern and sophisticated laboratory equipment that are able to carry out specialized tests in a much shorter period of time. The interior decoration is classical, with color tints that are relaxing and custom friendly to make visitors feel comfortable and at ease. Furthermore, hot and cold refreshments and snacks are available to all laboratory visitors in the Laboratory waiting room.
The majority of patients visiting the Laboratory facilities have been mostly referred hereto by their family doctors. But the Laboratory also provides services to specialist physicians, insurance companies, hotels, cruise ships, and private companies that require pre-employment drug screening for their new employees. However, in 2004 new requirements were introduced by the AZV (Algemene Ziekte Verzekering), the local health insurance company. As a requirement for the continuation of the contract with, for laboratory services to the local population, the laboratory firstly had to secure its accreditation to the ISO 15189. The laboratory was aware of the fact that this endeavor meant that staff and personnel had to dedicate huge amount of time and great efforts to attain the goal.
In March of 2008, the laboratory received the pleasant news of having been accredited to ISO 15189 by the American Association of Laboratory Accreditation (A2LA). This achievement not only signified a worldwide recognition for the I.L. Laboratorio Familiar N.V., but it also meant that the laboratory was in compliance with the requirements of the AZV. Now the laboratory was able to secure annual contracts with the AZV, one of the laboratory’s most important stakeholders and also the biggest purchaser of laboratory services.
This well-deserved achievement was the result of the enormous efforts undertaken by the staff and personnel of the laboratory during four long years. Many extra hours were devoted to labor and training that ultimately led to this accreditation. The IL Laboratory Familiar N.V. was not only the first laboratory on the island of Aruba but also the first laboratory in the western hemisphere to achieve the ISO 15189 accreditation. And also the first laboratory to be accredited by the American Association of Laboratory Accreditation (A2LA). Staff and personnel of the laboratory were extremely proud of their achievement.
With the accreditation came also the re-accreditation. And successively, in August 2010 and February 2013, the laboratory attained the ISO 15189 by A2LA and so maintaining full compliance with the ISO 15189 standards.
The Laboratory's success story is a result of the great team effort displayed by the different sections of the Laboratory and that has played a major role in the achievement of the established goals.
